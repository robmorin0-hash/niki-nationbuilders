Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Niki NationBuilders ("we", "us") publishes informational guides. This policy explains what data is collected when you visit niki-nationbuilders.ca and how it is used. We aim to collect as little as possible.

Information we collect directly

This is a static website. We do not run accounts, logins, or comment forms, and we do not ask you for personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Cookies and third-party services

Some data may be collected by third-party services we use:

  • Advertising (Google AdSense): if and when ads are enabled, Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits here and/or other sites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, and you can review your choices for third-party vendors at aboutads.info/choices and youronlinechoices.eu.
  • Affiliate links: some outbound product links are affiliate links. When you click one, the destination merchant or its affiliate network may set a cookie to attribute a possible purchase. We may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. We receive aggregate, not personally identifying, reporting from these programs.
  • Hosting (Netlify): our host may log standard technical request data (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability.

Analytics

We do not currently run a third-party analytics tracker. If we add one, this policy will be updated before it goes live.

Your choices

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings and use the advertising opt-out links above. Doing so will not break your ability to read the guides on this site.

Children

This site is intended for a general adult audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site's services change. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version.
