About Niki NationBuilders

Niki NationBuilders publishes practical, grounded guides on off-grid power, water, shelter, fabrication, food, and the engineering of self-reliant living.

Our guides are drafted with AI assistance and grounded in a primary-source research corpus, then reviewed before publishing. We aim for accurate and useful over fast and shallow. Where a claim goes beyond our sources we label it, and we prefer to omit a number we can't stand behind.
